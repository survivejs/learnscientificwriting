Description Scientific, or academic, writing sounds like something difficult and arcane. The purpose of this website is to demystify this complex topic and give you the tools you require to complete your research tasks. I have developed the resource to support my BSc/MSc students and included to it learnings from my own PhD process. My hope is that this resource can support you in your academic publishing efforts. The site has been structured as a book called "The Process of Scientific Writing". I have made the book freely available although I intend to offer a commercial version of it that you may optionally purchase in case it felt like the content was useful for you in a manner or another.
